Our Story

We would love to introduce ourselves.

Lunita Hermosa's grand opening was in 2020 and we continously provided our best effort for our customers.

We have a strong philosophy that food should provide a sense of comfort to forget our problems.

To enhance this experience, we have recently introduced table side aquariums to those who dine in our restaurant. These aquariums provide fresh air into our restaurant and of course provide a beautiful, calming view for you to enjoy

We hope this unique dining experience brings you as much joy as we love providing it!

Our Mission

In our restaurant, we strive to provide flavourful, and carefullly baked and cooked food in our menu. We love to see the joy our recipies have made on our community. Not only that, we acknowledge the unique taste and diets our loyal customers have. We refine our recipies so everyone, no matter our differences, can enjoy our food!

Customer Experience

The customer experience we provide is carefully considered. We hope to provide a cozy, dimly lit atmosphere in our restaurant to put our minds at ease and reduce overstimulation. Additionally, our staff openly cater to your concerns and feedback to further imporve our restaurant in regards to customer interaction and dietary concerns.
